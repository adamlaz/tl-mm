## 2. What this is — and what it isn’t

**It is**

- A **collaborative** look at execution: handoffs, tooling, process, and where friction shows up.
- **Systems-focused** — patterns across teams and tools, not scoring individuals.
- **Anonymous interviews** — I report themes, not who said what.

**It isn’t**

- A personnel review, performance evaluation, or blame exercise.
- An audit with pass/fail.
- A forum where individual comments get attributed by name.

If someone asks “Is this about me?” the answer is no. It’s about how the machine runs.
