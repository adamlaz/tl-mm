1 6 false default list true Summary This enhancement introduces a reliable modern_payment_id ↔ legacy_payment_id mapping mechanism to resolve transaction correlation failures for Cake POP payments. The solution consists of two independent flows: Express Sync Flow (Real-Time) Mapping row created during payment creation in Gateway. Scheduled PETL Export Flow (Asynchronous) Time-window based export of mapping rows to S3. Snowflake consumes from S3. This decouples payment processing from data export while ensuring accurate reconciliation. Problem Statement Cake POP (modern) payments: Submit to WorldPay before legacy payment records are generated. Contain modern payment ID. Do NOT contain the legacy payment_id in DiscretionaryField1. Legacy systems (EMAF, reconciliation logic) rely on: wide 760 Because no mapping existed: Transaction correlation failed. EMAF extraction logic broke. Solution Overview We introduced: A new table: gateway.modern_legacy_payment_mapping Real-time insertion of mapping during payment creation. A scheduled PETL export pipeline to S3. Snowflake consumption from S3.
